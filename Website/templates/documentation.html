Documentation


This is the page that we plan on posting our documentation of the building of this project. We want to guide you through how we went about creating this project -- our entire proxess from our successes to our setbacks. This project consists of several components: the video game itself, and this web application. Built in under 24 hours, this is a project that we can all confidently say is nothing like we have ever built before.















View our Documentation!

We made sure to document each step of the way while building our video game. We built the game from a place of empowerment and creativity -- Nova, our venturesome female protagonist, a citizen of the Earth's futuristic moon-colony, finds herself in a position where she must save the colony from color-stealing aliens; she must restore the color to the Moon, or remain in a grey world forever.


Eventually we plan on including a more detailed documentation of each component on this page, but we were unable to complete this in the 24 hours we were provided. However, you can still view our Google Doc of our raw and unedited component documentation in the link below!

Future Development Ideas!


New Frontiers


Super Nova on Earth

One of our future developmental ideas is to progress Nova to a new planet. After completing the Moon, she gains a "SuperNova" ability and title, where she can travel to other planets.

Super Nova to Mars

She first is able to travel to Earth, and once completed, Mars, as the blobs have moved on to infect these planets as well. Once she defeats the blobs in a planet, she can SuperNova to the next.
