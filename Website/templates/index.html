Welcome to Graffiti on the Moon!




Nova, our venturesome female protagonist, a citizen of the Earth's futuristic moon-colony, finds herself in a position where she must save the colony from color-stealing blobs. She must restore the color to the Moon, or remain in a grey world forever.
